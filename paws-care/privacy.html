Privacy Policy for Paws Care App

This Privacy Policy explains how Paws Care ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects the personal information of users ("you" or "your") who use our pet management application ("Paws Care App").

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you create an account or use certain features of the Paws Care App, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and pet information (e.g., name, breed, age).
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you interact with the Paws Care App, including actions you take within the app, device information (e.g., device type, operating system), and log data (e.g., IP address, access times).

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and Improve Services: We use the information we collect to provide and personalize the Paws Care App, including creating and managing pet profiles, tracking pet health data, and sending reminders and notifications.
  • Communicate with You: We may use your contact information to communicate with you about your account, provide customer support, and send important updates and announcements related to the Paws Care App.

Information Sharing

  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in providing and improving the Paws Care App (e.g., hosting services, analytics providers). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and only use it for specified purposes.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests (e.g., subpoenas, court orders), or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the security and integrity of your personal information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You can access, update, or delete your personal information by accessing your account settings within the Paws Care App. You may also choose to opt out of certain communications or data collection by adjusting your app settings.

Children's Privacy

The Paws Care App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Your continued use of the Paws Care App after any such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
